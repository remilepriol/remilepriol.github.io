Some projects I care about

DualityViz and DualSnakes are two interactive visualization projects I worked on during 2020's first lockdown. They are providing intuitions for convex duality. Convex duality is often presented through algebraic derivations, whereas it is rooted in beautiful geometric ideas (from projective geometry to be exact). My goal is to lay out these ideas. If you do not find the current outlook appealing, please reach out to me. I am needing help into making these graphs more easily accessible !

SDCA4CRF: During the year 2017 I worked on an optimization algorithm for a structured prediction model called CRF. Along the way I found out a way to systematically improve the convergence speed of the algorithm SDCA by sampling data points adaptively.

bitstring-knitting: a visual inspection of distances applied to bit strings.
